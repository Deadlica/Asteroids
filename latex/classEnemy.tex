\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classObject}{Object}} that represents the enemy boss. It inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classPlayer}{Player}} which inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classObject}{Object}}. It handles its own movement based on where the player is and attacks everytime its direction is changed.
